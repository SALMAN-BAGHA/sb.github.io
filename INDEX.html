ALL ABOUT US

Located in Karachi,Pakistan STAR SPORTS CLUB provides regulation courts and playing
fields for more than a dozen sports in one convenient location. We offer climate controlled indoor sports,outdoor sports and fitness facilities,
as well as both natural and synthetic outdoor fields for year-round use. Stop by for a free visit to see our first-class
facilities and meet our friendly, professional staff.

FACILITIES AVAILABLE

As the most innovative sports facility in Karachi, STAR SPORTS CLUB is the premier destination for
individuals, families, athletes, and businesses. With more than 700,000 square feet of usable space under one roof, our unique capacity
creates an unparalleled experience for our members and guests.
